
# Coffee Shop Digital Presence Audit

## ☕ Overview
A marketing audit evaluating a local coffee shop’s digital presence and customer engagement.

## 🔍 What was analyzed
- Customer profile
- Social media activity
- Online reviews & ratings
- Digital visibility
- Marketing gaps

## 📊 Outcome
Identified key opportunities to improve visibility, engagement, and customer reach.

## 🛠 Methods Used
- Brand audit framework
- Owner interview analysis
- Digital presence evaluation

## 🎯 Purpose
To apply marketing strategy and research techniques to a real business scenario.
